Things I like to do

Listening to music helps me a lot. It distracts me whenever I have bad thoughts.
I believe music has the ability to convey all sorts of emotion.
Whether the emotion is Joy and happiness or sadness and despair through the rhythms, harmonies and the lyrics.
Music has the ability to transport me back in time Just like a time machine.
It lets me revisit lost and favorite moments in life.
